Interpol

Interpol is a renowned American rock band formed in New York City in 1997. Known for their post-punk revival sound characterized by atmospheric guitars, driving basslines, and introspective lyrics, Interpol quickly gained recognition with their debut album "Turn on the Bright Lights." Over the years, they have established themselves as one of the most influential bands of the early 2000s indie rock scene.
